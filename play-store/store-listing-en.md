## Full Description (4000 characters max)
Experience the joy of language exchange with Nurio!

Nurio connects you with weekly language exchange meetups in Seoul and Daejeon, South Korea. Practice natural conversations with native speakers, improve your language skills, and make friends from diverse cultural backgrounds.

### Key Features

- Find Weekly Language Exchange Events
Discover various language exchange meetups happening every week in Seoul and Daejeon. View location, date, and participation fees at a glance.

- Easy Event Registration
Found a meetup you like? Complete your registration with just a few taps.

- Profile Management
Set your language level and interests, and meet participants with similar backgrounds.

- Real-time Updates
Receive important notifications about new events, registration confirmations, and schedule changes in real-time.

### Perfect For

- Those who want to practice English conversation with native speakers
- People looking to make international friends
- Anyone wanting to improve language skills through free talking
- Those interested in experiencing diverse cultures
- People in the Seoul/Daejeon area

### How to Join

1. Install the app and set up your profile
2. Browse and select an event you like
3. Complete your registration
4. Meet new friends at the meetup venue!
